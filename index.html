Welcome To BTS

BTS ( Korean : 방탄소년단 ; RR : Bangtan Sonyeondan ) is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. The group consists of seven members: Jin , Suga , J-Hope , RM , Jimin , V , and Jungkook. The members of the group co-write and co-produce their songs based on their identities. Originally a hip hop group , their music has evolved into a variety of genres. Their lyrics often deal with mental health, teenage-school issues, coming of age , loss, self-love, and individualism. Their music also frequently references literature, philosophy, psychological concepts, and parallel universe themes./p>
